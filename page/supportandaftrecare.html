Our Support & Aftercare

Earcanhear cares about the people who choose to associate themselves to our services. The moment our expert Vijay audiologists prescribe a hearing aid for your individual needs, along comes a free aftercare program for a lifetime. We ensure that you being a user of hearing aid must get the maximum benefits of the latest developments in this field and the world around you should always sound memorable and dramatic.

With Earcanhear, it is not a kind of deliver and forget process, instead we believe in making relationships at every step.

Features of Free Lifetime After-Care Program

Assessment of Hearing Health
The individual needs may differ from time to time due to multiple factors (like age, stress, climate, etc.) Expert audiologists ensure timely check-up of your ears to escape unnecessary discomfort. Our pro-active approach is the core characteristic of our aftercare program.
Performance Evaluation of Hearing Aid Equipment
Earcanhear audiologists help you in understanding your hearing aid / equipment and simultaneously advise you on taking the important measures for the best regular care of it. We want to maximize the performance of the products we advocate and simultaneously give perfect results to our customers. These instrument care processes will make your device last longer and give optimized performance. From general cleaning to battery replacement, we help you understand it all.
Awareness about Technology Innovations
Our program also includes demonstrations on the new technologies in place, new products and other forms of help and advice. Earcanhear’s support is a long run aspect; we give a new form of life and we want you to experience it to the fullest
Earcanhear’s Free Lifetime After-Care program is a regular program for the evaluation and assessment of your hearing health and the performance of your equipment. Further, the 5-star Service Check ensures that your products perform with their maximum efficiency.​
